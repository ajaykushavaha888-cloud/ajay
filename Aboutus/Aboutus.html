Welcome to SpacECE India Foundation!

At SpacECE, we believe that education is the key to unlocking a brighter future. We are a non-profit organization dedicated to providing quality education, promoting lifelong learning, and empowering individuals and communities across India. Our mission is to bridge the educational gap and create equal opportunities for all. We envision a society where every child has access to quality education, regardless of their background or circumstances. Through our initiatives, we strive to inspire, empower, and transform lives. With a team of passionate educators, volunteers, and partners, we work tirelessly to make a positive impact on education. Our programs focus on various aspects, including early childhood education, school support, vocational training, and capacity-building for parents and teachers. Through our Home-as-a-Learning-SPACE initiative, we aim to transform homes into vibrant learning environments. We provide resources, guidance, and support to parents, empowering them to actively engage in their child's education and create a nurturing atmosphere for learning. We also establish learning centers in underserved communities, equipped with educational materials, technology, and qualified educators. These centers serve as safe and inclusive spaces where children can thrive academically and develop essential life skills. At SpacECE, we believe in the power of collaboration and partnerships. We actively collaborate with schools, government agencies, corporate entities, and other non-profit organizations to amplify our impact and reach more beneficiaries. Together, we can create a powerful ecosystem that nurtures educational excellence and social change. We invite you to join us in our mission to transform lives through education. Whether you are an individual, a corporate entity, or a passionate volunteer, there are many ways to get involved. Your support, be it through donations, volunteering, or advocacy, can make a significant difference in shaping a brighter future for countless individuals. Explore our website to learn more about our programs, initiatives, and success stories. Together, let's empower individuals, strengthen communities, and build a nation where education is a fundamental right for all. Thank you for visiting SpacECE India Foundation. We look forward to embarking on this transformative journey with you.

Legal Status

SpacECE, registered as SpacECE India Foundation under section 8 of the Companies Act 2013, holds a solid legal status. The organization possesses various important documents and certificates, including the Memorandum of Association (MOA) and Articles of Association (AOA) registered with the office of the Registrar of Companies. Additionally, SpacECE is equipped with the necessary registrations such as the Permanent Account Number (PAN), Tax Deduction Account Number (TAN), 12A, and 80G which grants exemption from income tax payment. Furthermore, SpacECE is recognized by the government of NGO Darpan, confirming its status as a recognized non-profit organization.
